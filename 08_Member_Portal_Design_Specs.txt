SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Platform: Web-based (responsive) + Native Mobile Apps (iOS/Android)
Backend: Node.js + PostgreSQL
Frontend: React.js
Payment Processing: Stripe
Booking Engine: Custom-built on top of CourseReserve API
Authentication: OAuth 2.0 + JWT tokens
Hosting: AWS (scalable infrastructure)

BOOKING RULES ENGINE

Priority Booking:
- Bronze: 48 hours advance
- Silver: 48 hours advance
- Gold: 7 days advance
- Platinum: 14 days advance

Booking Limits:
- Maximum bookings per member: 3 concurrent
- Maximum hours per week: 10 hours (adjustable)
- No-show penalties: 3 no-shows = 30-day booking restriction

Dynamic Pricing:
- Peak hours (Mon-Fri 5-9pm, Sat-Sun all day): +25%
- Off-peak (Mon-Fri 11am-5pm): Standard
- Late night (after 9pm): -15%

Credit System:
- 1 credit = $10 value
- Credits auto-apply at checkout
- Remaining balance charged to payment method
- Credits expire 60 days from cancellation

Cancellation Policy:
- 48+ hours: Full credit refund
- 24-48 hours: 50% credit refund
- < 24 hours: No refund
- No-show: No refund + penalty
